KeepScore - Tine Minte Scorul
Aplicatie usor de utilizat pentru Android

Trebuie sa tii scorul? Arunca pixul si hartia!

KeepScore este o aplicatie pentru scor gratuita si open-source care poate fi folosita pentru orice joc bazat pe puncte.I'm a board gamer, so I wrote it to help me and my friends when we play games together.

Features:

- Salveaza scorul automat

- Shows a complete scoring history of every game

- History automatically updates after 10 seconds (configurable)

- Mentine ecranul deschis

- Permite adaugarea a 2-8 jucatori

- Saves multiple games

- Long-press on a button to add/subtract different values

- Configure the add/subtract buttons

- Add/edit/remove players during play

- Backup/restore to SD card

Enjoy!
